## The case for Stellar

When institutions ask us which chain to build their tokenization infrastructure on, the answer is almost always Stellar, and for good reason.

Stellar was designed from the ground up for financial applications. Its built-in compliance primitives, asset issuance mechanics, and fast settlement times make it uniquely suited for real-world asset tokenization.

## Compliance built in

Unlike general-purpose smart contract platforms, Stellar has native support for authorization flags, clawback mechanisms, and regulated asset flows. This means you don't need to bolt on compliance after the fact, it's part of the protocol.

## Speed and cost

Stellar settles transactions in 3-5 seconds with fees measured in fractions of a cent. For high-volume tokenization use cases, whether equities, bonds, or real estate, this matters enormously.

## The institutional momentum

The Stellar Development Foundation has made institutional adoption a core priority. With partnerships across fintech, payments, and capital markets, the ecosystem is growing fast.

We've been building on Stellar since its smart contract evolution, and the developer experience keeps getting better.
