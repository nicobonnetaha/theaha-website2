## Overview

Authline is an institutional-grade dApp for managing Stellar assets, trustlines, and account authorization at scale. It gives operations teams a clean interface to perform actions that previously required raw transaction construction or deep SDK knowledge.

## The problem

As more financial institutions deploy tokenized assets on Stellar, a new operational challenge has emerged: managing the lifecycle of those assets across hundreds or thousands of accounts.

Issuing a regulated asset on Stellar involves setting authorization flags, managing trustline approvals, controlling clawback rights, and coordinating multi-sig operations. Doing this at institutional scale, quickly, correctly, and with an audit trail, was painful without the right tooling.

Authline was built to solve exactly that.

## What we built

### Asset management dashboard

A clean, multi-asset dashboard that gives operators a real-time view of all assets under management, total supply, holder distribution, pending authorizations, and freeze status.

### Trustline authorization at scale

Authline lets issuers approve or revoke trustlines in bulk, with filtering by account status, KYC tier, and jurisdiction. What used to require scripting is now a few clicks with full confirmation flows.

### Authorization flag controls

Operators can manage the full set of Stellar authorization flags, AUTH_REQUIRED, AUTH_REVOCABLE, AUTH_CLAWBACK_ENABLED, directly from the UI, with change history logged for audit purposes.

### Clawback management

Regulated asset issuers can initiate clawback transactions from the dashboard, with multi-step confirmation to prevent accidental execution. All clawback events are logged with reason codes for regulatory reporting.

### Multi-sig coordination

For institutions that require multi-party approval on sensitive operations, Authline integrates with Stellar's multi-sig primitives to coordinate approvals across signers, without requiring all parties to have deep blockchain knowledge.

## Who it's for

Authline is designed for the compliance and operations teams at asset issuers, not just developers. The interface abstracts away the underlying Stellar transaction mechanics while preserving full control and transparency.

## Open source

Like Scaffold Stellar, Authline is open source. We believe the tooling layer should be shared infrastructure for the ecosystem, the institutions building on top of it can differentiate on their products, not their internal tooling.
